# Purpose

## Mission

**Transform raw research into compelling narratives in seconds.**

conclusiv eliminates the hours spent structuring and formatting presentations, letting professionals focus on what matters: their insights and message.

## Vision

A world where anyone can communicate complex ideas clearly and beautifully, regardless of design skills or presentation experience.

## Core User

**Business professionals** who:
- Conduct research and analysis
- Need to communicate findings to stakeholders
- Don't have time to craft presentations manually
- Want professional-quality output without design expertise

### User Personas

1. **The Analyst** - Has data and insights, needs to present to leadership
2. **The Consultant** - Compiles research, delivers to clients
3. **The Founder** - Has a vision, needs to pitch to investors
4. **The Researcher** - Has findings, needs to share with teams
5. **The Executive** - Receives briefings, needs crisp summaries
6. **The Product Manager** - Has roadmaps, needs stakeholder alignment

## Key Value Propositions

| Value | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Speed** | From research to presentation in under 60 seconds |
| **Quality** | AI-generated narratives with professional structure |
| **Personalization** | Tailored to your company's voice and brand |
| **Accessibility** | Voice-first input means no keyboard required |
| **Simplicity** | 4-step flow: Input → Refine → Preview → Present |
| **Intelligence** | 7 phases of narrative intelligence for optimal output |
| **Verification** | AI-powered fact checking for claim accuracy |
| **Flexibility** | Multiple audience modes and narrative archetypes |

## Intelligence Phases

conclusiv applies 7 progressive intelligence phases:

| Phase | Feature | Value |
|-------|---------|-------|
| 1 | Audience Mode | Adapt content for executives, investors, product teams, etc. |
| 2 | Tension Detector | Identify contradictions and blind spots before presenting |
| 3 | Narrative Archetypes | Apply proven formats (strategy brief, pitch, etc.) |
| 4 | Adaptive Compression | Control duration from 10 seconds to full presentation |
| 5 | View Modes | Present live, share as reader view, or export |
| 6 | Company Brain | Apply historical context and patterns |
| 7 | Alternative Narratives | Generate different framings for different goals |

## What conclusiv Does

- ✅ **Generates narratives** - Structured, professional presentations from raw input
- ✅ **Extracts themes** - Identifies 3-7 key themes with priority levels
- ✅ **Extracts claims** - Pulls out assertions for review and verification
- ✅ **Verifies facts** - AI-powered claim verification with confidence scores
- ✅ **Detects tensions** - Finds contradictions and blind spots
- ✅ **Adapts to audience** - Tailors content for specific stakeholders
- ✅ **Applies archetypes** - Uses proven narrative structures
- ✅ **Compresses intelligently** - Creates 10-second to full-length versions
- ✅ **Generates alternatives** - Creates different framings for different goals
- ✅ **Exports professionally** - PDF and PowerPoint generation
- ✅ **Integrates branding** - Logo, colors, and fonts from URL or upload

## What conclusiv Doesn't Do

- **Not a slide deck tool** - We generate narratives, not static slides
- **Not a design tool** - AI handles styling automatically
- **Not collaborative editing** - Single-user workflow (collaboration planned)
- **Not a data visualization tool** - Focused on narrative, not charts
- **Not a research platform** - Augments research, doesn't replace it

## Guiding Principles

1. **Voice-first, not voice-only** - Support multiple input methods
2. **AI-augmented, human-controlled** - Users can always edit and verify
3. **Beautiful by default** - Every output looks professional
4. **Progressive disclosure** - Simple by default, powerful when needed
5. **Speed without sacrifice** - Fast generation with high quality
6. **Trust through transparency** - Show confidence scores, cite sources
7. **Mobile-native** - First-class mobile experience with haptics
